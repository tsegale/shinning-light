About Us

Founded in 2014 and led by Mrs. Pashukeni Tuyeimo Johannes (Finance Management degree), Shining-Light Investments CC is a proudly Namibian close corporation. We serve government ministries, parastatals, NGOs, and private clients with a culture of transparency, quality, and continuous staff development.

Registered in Windhoek and operating across Namibia, we have catered over 500 people, held multi-year government contracts, and built a diverse portfolio spanning food, cleaning, crafts, natural oils, and livestock identification.
