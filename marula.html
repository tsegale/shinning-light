Natural Production

Pure Marula Oil,
straight from nature.

We produce cold-pressed Marula oil extracted from the kernels of the Sclerocarya birrea — the Marula tree native to Southern Africa. The result is a clear, golden-brown botanical oil packed with antioxidants, essential fatty acids, Vitamin C, and phenolic compounds. 100% natural, proudly Namibian.

Our oil is available wholesale for cosmetic manufacturers, food producers, and retailers. We currently supply Tateii Food Producer CC on an ongoing basis and welcome new partnerships.

Skin Care Ideal for oily, acne-prone, dry, and aging skin. Lightweight and non-greasy, it absorbs rapidly and leaves no residue. Rich in Oleic and Linoleic acid to restore the skin barrier.
Hair Treatment Nourishes root to tip. Hydrating, moisturising, and occlusive — outstanding for dry, frizzy, or chemically treated hair. A few drops of Marula oil dramatically improve lustre and manageability.
Culinary & Food Use A delicacy in traditional and modern African cooking. Marula oil has been used for centuries to flavour food and preserve meat for up to a year without refrigeration. High smoke point makes it suitable for frying.

How It's Made

Our production process.

Every bottle of Shining-Light Marula Oil follows a careful, traditional cold-press process to preserve all the natural nutrients and beneficial compounds.

Harvesting
Ripe Marula fruits are collected from wild trees in the Namibian bush after they have naturally fallen. Only mature, undamaged fruit is selected.
Kernel Extraction
The outer flesh is removed and the hard inner nut is cracked open by hand to reveal the white kernel inside — the source of the oil.
Cold Pressing
Dried kernels are cold-pressed without heat or chemical solvents. This preserves all antioxidants, fatty acids, and Vitamin C in their natural state.
Filtering & Quality Check
The raw oil is carefully filtered to remove any sediment, then quality-checked for colour, clarity, and aroma before bottling or bulk supply.
