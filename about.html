Our Story

Proudly Namibian since 2014.

A close corporation built on transparency, commitment, and a mission to make quality services accessible to all Namibians.

"To help establish a society where all people have access to the basic services that develop and upgrade the lives of the Namibian nation at large."

— Our Mission Statement

Why We Exist

Every service Shining-Light offers — from catering a government function to supplying a farmer with ear tags — is rooted in this mission. We believe that quality, consistency, and accessibility should not be reserved for a few.

Since our founding, we have built lasting relationships with clients across the public and private sector, earned multi-year contracts through demonstrated reliability, and grown our portfolio without ever losing sight of why we started.

Our History

How we got here.

Shining-Light Investments CC was established in 2014 under the Close Corporations Act (Registration No. CC/2014/10999). From the outset, the company was built around a broad but purposeful mandate: general trading and investments, roadwork, manufacturing, produce, cleaning, catering, construction, hardware, property development, and import and export.

Over the years, we sharpened our focus to seven core service areas where we consistently deliver the best value — catering, cleaning, construction, animal ear tags, Marula oil production, tailoring and crocheting, and corporate goods supply.

Today Shining-Light serves government ministries, parastatals, NGOs, and private businesses across Namibia — not as a contractor, but as a trusted partner.

What We Stand For

Our organisational values.

Transparency

We are honest and open in all situations. Everyone has the opportunity to freely express their thoughts, fully respecting the opinions of others, with an attitude that is polite and constructive. Work is undertaken through initiative, commitment, and motivation.

Corporate Culture

Our culture is built on innovative ideas where everyone is involved and contributing. Service excellence is measured not just by standards and procedures, but by initiative and commitment. Every individual role is crucial — whether client-facing or behind the scenes.

HR Development

We are committed to the continued development and training of our people. Everyone's accountability and diligence make it possible to achieve performances of excellence that impact positively on the customer's experience. Our team is our greatest asset.

Leadership

Founder & Director.

Shining-Light Investments CC is privately owned and led by Mrs. Pashukeni Tuyeimo Johannes, the company's sole member and director.

Mrs. Pashukeni brings over five years of professional experience as a Restaurant Finance Manager, combined with a degree in Finance Management. This background informs her disciplined approach to running operations — from managing multi-year catering contracts to overseeing the company's growth into construction and corporate supply.

Under her leadership, Shining-Light has built relationships with government ministries, parastatals, and NGOs — growing steadily while keeping service quality and client trust at the centre of every decision.
