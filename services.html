Catering & Restaurant

"Great Food. Great Service. Spectacular Events!"

From intimate coffee breaks to large-scale 500-person events, Shining-Light provides exceptional African and European cuisine prepared fresh from scratch. We hold a 3-year catering contract with AMTA and have catered events for multiple government ministries, parastatals, and NGOs across Namibia.

Whether it's a formal state function, a corporate conference, a wedding reception, or a community gathering, our team ensures every dish is served with care and consistency. We are also part artists — food is crafted to fit both your event and your budget.

Livestock

Animal Ear Tags

We supply fully customised polyurethane livestock ear tags printed with the farmer's name, farm registration number, and contact details. Our tags are designed for Namibia's demanding outdoor environment — UV-resistant, durable, and legible for the life of the animal.

Available in a full range of colours and sizes to suit cattle, goats, sheep, and small stock. We supply matching applicator pins and bulk packs for commercial farms and communal farmers alike.

Natural · 100% Namibian

Marula Oil Production

We produce pure cold-pressed Marula oil extracted from the kernels of the Marula tree — a clear, golden-brown botanical oil celebrated across Southern Africa. Our oil is rich in antioxidants, Oleic acid, Linoleic acid, Vitamin C, and phenolic compounds.

Supplied wholesale to cosmetic manufacturers and food producers. We have an ongoing supply agreement with Tateii Food Producer CC and welcome enquiries from retailers and exporters.
