Catering & Restaurant

From intimate 2-person coffee breaks to large-scale 500-person events, Shining-Light provides exceptional African and European cuisine prepared fresh from scratch. We hold a 3-year catering contract with AMTA and have catered events for multiple government ministries, parastatals, and NGOs.

Whether it's a formal state function, a corporate conference, a wedding reception, or a community gathering, our team ensures every dish is served with care and consistency.

Cleaning Services

We deliver industrial, commercial, and residential cleaning with a culture of pride in performance. Our teams are trained to maintain the highest hygiene standards and take genuine ownership of every space they service.

Services include floor care, window cleaning, deep-cleaning, waste removal, and supply of hygiene consumables. We have cleaned for government ministry offices and have served the Ministry of Land Reform with both cleaning and stationery supply.

Tailoring & Crocheting

We design and produce custom garments for individuals and organisations — from tailored corporate uniforms and staff workwear to traditional and contemporary clothing. Our handcrafted crochet range includes blankets, baby sets, and home décor items.

Visit us at Stall S3 at the Windhoek Flea Market, or contact us directly for bulk corporate orders. We have supplied corporate wear for the Game Product Trust Fund.

Livestock

Animal Ear Tags

We supply fully customised polyurethane livestock ear tags printed with the farmer's name, farm registration number, and contact details. Our tags are designed for Namibia's demanding outdoor environment — UV-resistant, durable, and legible for the life of the animal.

Available in a full range of colours and sizes to suit cattle, goats, sheep, and small stock. We supply matching applicator pins and bulk packs for commercial farms and communal farmers alike.

Marula Oil Production

We produce pure cold-pressed Marula oil extracted from the kernels of the Marula tree — a clear, golden-brown botanical oil celebrated across Southern Africa. Our oil is rich in antioxidants, Oleic acid, Linoleic acid, Vitamin C, and phenolic compounds.

Supplied wholesale to cosmetic manufacturers and food producers. We have an ongoing supply agreement with Tateii Food Producer CC and welcome enquiries from retailers and exporters.
